Bukbuz, Inc. is a locally owned bookstore based in New York City. Currently Bukbuz, Inc. has more than 50,000 ready to ship books. It covers an area of about 10,000 square feet and two floors. It also hosts more than five author appearances per month. Bukbuz, Inc. has had the distinction of receiving the prestigious “Best Book Store in New York” award five times in a row.
